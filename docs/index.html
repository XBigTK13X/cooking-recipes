Apple Crisp

  • 3/4 cup ap flour
  • 4 apples (thinly sliced)
  • 1/3 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Grease an 8x8x2 inch pan
  3. Spread apple slices evenly throughout pan
  4. Mix water, cinnamon, and salt in a bowl
  5. Pour cinnamon mixture over apples
  6. Mix sugar, flour, and butter in a bowl
  7. Spread crumb mixture over the apples
  8. Bake for 40 minutes or until crumb is golden brown

Blond Brownies

  • 2 and 3/4 cups ap flour
  • 2 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 pound brown sugar
  • 12 ounces chocolate chips
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup shortening
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  1. Heat oven to 325
  2. Cream the shortening, brown sugar, and eggs in a mixer
  3. Mix in the flour, baking powder, salt, vanilla, and chocolate chips
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown

Easy Oreo Cheesecake

  • 1/4 cup butter (melted)
  • 32 ounces cream cheese (softened)
  • 4 eggs
  • 18 ounces oreo sandwich cookies
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  1. Preheat oven to 325
  2. Pulse 30 oreo cookies in a food processor until finely ground
  3. Pulse in the melted butter to form a crumb
  4. Line a 13x9 inch baking pan with aluminum foil, leave some foil hanging over the edges of the pan
  5. Firmly press oreo crust into bottom of greased baking pan
  6. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until well blended
  7. Mix sour cream into cream cheese mixture
  8. Mix in eggs one at a time
  9. Chop remaining oreo cookies
  10. Gently fold 1 and 1/2 cups of chopped cookies into the cream cheese mixture
  11. Pour mixture into pan with the crust
  12. Sprinkle pan with the remaining chopped cookies
  13. Bake for 45 minutes or until the center is almost set
  14. Let the cheesecake cool and then refrigerate for at least 3 hours, preferabley overnight

Strawberry Swirl Cheesecake

  • 3 tablespoons ap flour
  • 3 tablespoons butter (melted)
  • 40 ounces cream cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup honey graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup seedless strawberry jam
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  1. Preheat oven to 325
  2. Line a 13x9 baking pan with foil, hanging some over the edges
  3. Mix together crumbs, 3 tablespoons of sugar, and butter
  4. Press the crumb mixture into the bottom of the baking pan
  5. Beat cream cheese, remaining sugar, flour, and vanilla
  6. Mix in sour cream after other ingredients are blended
  7. Mix in one egg at a time
  8. Pour cream cheese mixture over crust in baking pan
  9. Gently drop spoonfuls of jam over the top of the batter
  10. Run a dull knife through the mixture several times to create a marbling effect
  11. Bake for 40 minutes or until the center is almost set
  12. Cool and then refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight
