Alton Brown's Southern Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces ap flour
  • 4 teaspoons powder of baking
  • 1/4 teaspoon soda of baking
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 ounce butter
  • 2 ounces shortening (crisco)
  • 8 ounces buttermilk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Whisk together the dry ingredients
  3. Cut in the shortening and butter using a whisk until you have coarse crumbs
  4. Make a well and combine the buttermilk and the dry ingredients with a spoon
  5. Knead the dough in the bowl with your hands until no loose flour remains in the bowl
  6. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and gently knead it for a minute or so, until you have folded it and the dough is consistent
  7. Roll out the dough, then cut out as many biscuits rounds as you can, then fold the dough and repeat the process until no dough remains
  8. Place the cut out biscuits onto a biscuit sheet so that they barely touch
  9. Bake for 20 minutes

Apple Crisp

Ingredients

  • 4 apples (thinly sliced)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup ap flour
  • 1/3 cup butter (softened)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Grease an 8x8x2 inch pan
  3. Spread apple slices evenly throughout pan
  4. Mix water, cinnamon, and salt in a bowl
  5. Pour cinnamon mixture over apples
  6. Mix sugar, flour, and butter in a bowl
  7. Spread crumb mixture over the apples
  8. Bake for 40 minutes or until crumb is golden brown

Apple Sauce Cake

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon soda of baking
  • 1 cup sour apple sauce (cold)
  • 1 and 3/4 cup ap flour
  • 2 teaspoon water (warm)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300

  3. Dissolve soda of baking into warm water

  6. Stir in the flour

Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 400 grams (4-5 bananas) banana (over ripened)
  • 113 grams (1 stick) unsalted butter (melted)
  • 50 grams brown sugar
  • 150 grams white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 235 grams ap flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon soda of baking
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 90 grams pecan
  • 90 grams chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Grease a 9x5x3 loaf pan. Line it with parchment, then grease the parchment
  3. In a large bowl, mash the bananas with the butter, sugars, eggs, and vanilla
  4. In another bowl, stir together the ap flour, soda of baking, salt, and cinnamon
  5. Combine the nuts and chocolate chips, separate 1/4 of the mixture
  6. Gently fold the wet into the dry
  7. Gently fold in the 3/4 portion of mixins
  8. Pour the batter into the loaf pan
  9. Sprinkle the remaining 1/4 mixins over the top of the batter
  10. Bake until the center temps between 200 and 205 degrees (a little over an hour)
  11. Let the load rest in it's pan for 15-20 minutes, then use the parchment to move it to a cooling rack

Notes

Modified from https://cafedelites.com/banana-bread/

Chocolate Chunk Cookies

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 200 grams (1 cup) light brown sugar
  • 20 grams (1 tablespoon) molasses
  • 50 grams (1/4 cups) white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 3 grams (1 teaspoon) vanilla
  • 250 grams (1 1/2 cups) ap flour
  • 5 grams (1 teaspoon) soda of baking
  • 4 grams (1/2 teaspoon) salt
  • 4 ounces 60%-70% high quality dark chocolate (roughly chopped)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Brown the butter on the stove
  3. Pour the browned butter into a large bowl
  4. Whisk brown sugar, white sugar, and molasses into the browned butter
  5. Whisking the sugars should cool the butter enough that it won't scramble the eggs
  6. Whisk in egg, extra egg yolk, and vanilla
  7. Combine the ap flour, soda of baking, salt in a separate bowl
  8. Using a rubber spatula, fold the dry into the wet until just combined
  9. Fold in the chopped dark chocolate
  10. Divide dough into 18 portions (approximately 45 grams per cookie) across three lined sheet pans
  11. Try to get larger chunks of chocolate to face upward to improve final appearance
  12. Bake for 11 minutes, or until the edges start to brown
  13. Remove from the oven and place on a parchment lined cooling rack until cool

Chocolate Vanilla Swirl Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 20 cookies oreo
  • 3 tablespoon butter (melted)
  • 32 ounces cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 squares semi-sweet baking chocolate (melted)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325
  2. Line a 13x9 baking pan with foil, leaving some foil hanging over the edge of the pan
  3. Mix cookie crumbs and butter
  4. Press crumb mixture into bottom of pan
  5. Bake for 10 minutes
  6. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until well blended
  7. Mix in sour cream
  8. Mix eggs in one at a time
  9. Set aside 1 cup of the cream cheese batter
  10. Mix melted chocolate into remaining batter
  11. Pour chocolate batter over the cooked crust
  12. Drop spoonfuls of set aside batter into the pan
  13. Cut through the batters multiple times using a dull knife to create a marbling effect
  14. Bake for 40 minutes or until the center is almost set
  15. Cool and then refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight

Crepe

Ingredients

  • 2 large egg
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter (melted)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs and milk together
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and whisk until smooth
  3. Refrigerate at least one hour
  4. Heat a 10 inch nonstick skillet over medium
  5. Lightly grease with butter, wipe off excess
  6. If the batter is too thick to pour, thin it out with either milk or melted butter
  7. Hold the pan and pour in 1/3 cup of batter
  8. Immediately swirl the batter to evenly coat the bottom in a paper thin layer
  9. Cook until the top look dry, should take around 20 seconds
  10. Grab one edge of the crepe and cook on the second side for 10 seconds, or until edges start to brown

Notes

Based on Kenji's crêpe recipe

Diner Style Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ap flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon soda of baking
  • 1/2 teaspoon powder of baking
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 4 tablespoon butter (melted)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix all of the dry ingredients together.
  2. Preheat a griddle to 325
  3. Beat the egg white, buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and oil
  4. THoroughly mix the wet ingredients into the dry.
  5. Using a paper towel, spread a pad of butter on the griddle to create a barely visible coating of fat

Easy Oreo Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 18 ounces oreo sandwich cookies
  • 1/4 cup butter (melted)
  • 32 ounces cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325
  2. Pulse 30 oreo cookies in a food processor until finely ground
  3. Pulse in the melted butter to form a crumb
  4. Line a 13x9 inch baking pan with aluminum foil, leave some foil hanging over the edges of the pan
  5. Firmly press oreo crust into bottom of greased baking pan
  6. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until well blended
  7. Mix sour cream into cream cheese mixture
  8. Mix in eggs one at a time
  9. Chop remaining oreo cookies
  10. Gently fold 1 and 1/2 cups of chopped cookies into the cream cheese mixture
  11. Pour mixture into pan with the crust
  12. Sprinkle pan with the remaining chopped cookies
  13. Bake for 45 minutes or until the center is almost set
  14. Let the cheesecake cool and then refrigerate for at least 3 hours, preferabley overnight

Kenji's New York Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 450 grams bread flour
  • 10 grams white sugar
  • 10 grams salt
  • 10 grams active dry yeast
  • 20 grams extra virgin olive oil
  • 280 grams water (warm)

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients in a KitchenAid mixer on low with a dough hook
  2. Add the wet ingredients and mix on low until combined
  3. Scrape the sides of the bowl and knead on high speed for 10 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth to the touch
  4. Store the dough in an oiled bowl
  5. Refrigerate the dough at least overnight, best after two to three days.
  6. When using the dough, make sure to allow it to come up to room temperature before working, at least an hour or two

Notes

Modified from Serious Eats.

Kenji's Pie Crust

Ingredients

  • 100 grams (1/2 cup) vegetable shortening (cold)
  • 170 grams (1 1/2 cups) unsalted butter (cold)
  • 360 grams ap flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/3 cup vodka
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 handful ice cubes

Steps

  1. Mix the ap flour, salt, and sugar in a large bowl
  2. Cut the shortening and butter together into the dry ingredients until they form crumbs
  3. Combine the ice cubes, water, and vodka

Linzer Cookies

Ingredients

  • 226 gram butter
  • 339 gram ap flour
  • 113 gram powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 egg yolk
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 2 teaspoon (1 lemon) lemon zest
  • 12 oz seedless fruit jam (raspberry, apricot, etc)

Steps

  1. Combine the salt and flour in a bowl
  2. Cream the butter and powdered sugar together
  3. Mix egg yolk, vanilla extract, almond extract, and lemon zest into the creamed butter
  4. Fold the dry ingredients into the wet
  5. Tightly wrap the dough in plastic wrap
  6. Chill for at least two hours
  7. Remove the dough from the fridge
  8. Preheat the oven to 350
  9. Gently roll out on a floured surface to 1 cm thickness
  10. Cut the dough into 3 inch rounds
  11. Using a 1 inch cutter, remove the center from half of the rounds
  12. Reroll scraps and continue process until all usable cookies are shaped
  13. Bake the cookies for 14 minutes
  14. Move cookies to a cooling rack and allow to cool completely
  15. Spread two teaspoons of jam on each base cookie, top with the ring cookie
  16. Cookies can be enjoyed immediately, but improve with an overnight rest to allow absorption of jam moisture

Pillows

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup splenda zero
  • 9 oz skim milk greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups ap flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon soda of baking
  • mixins (see step 1)

Steps

  1. The mixins will determine what kind of pillow you make. My favorite is 2 cups of frozen blueberries. We have also had success with 1/2 cup of mini chocolate chips. Anything you would use to flavor a muffin should work great
  2. Preheat oven to 400
  3. In a bowl, stir the flour, soda of baking, and salt together
  4. In a separate large bowl, whisk the splenda and eggs together until splenda is evenly distributed
  5. Whisk in the vanilla, applesauce, and greek yogurt
  6. Using a spatula, gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet mix. Do not overwork the batter.
  7. Gently fold the mixins into the batter
  8. Using an ice cream disher, drop 12 gobs of batter onto two foiled and greased sheet pans
  9. Bake until the pillows are just starting to turn golden (15 to 20 minutes, depending on the choice of mixins)
  10. Move the cooked pillows to a wire rack
  11. Once cooled, store in refrigerator for up to one week.
  12. Before serving, toast the pillow to reconstitute it.

Stove Top White Rice

Ingredients

  • 200 grams white rice (jasmine)
  • 300 grams water
  • 2 pinches salt

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice until cold water until the excess starch is rinsed off and the water runs clear
  2. During the rinse, the rice will absorb about 20% of its mass in water. This doesn't count toward the measurement in the recipe
  3. Without too rigorously shaking the rice, so that it retains the absorbed water, dump it into a pot
  4. Stir the measure of water and the pinches of salt into the pot
  5. Bring the pot, uncovered, to a boil
  6. Immediately lower the heat until it is barely simmering and cover
  7. After 15 minutes, quickly check the rice. Scoot a little aside to see if any water remains in the pot.
  8. Once no water remains, drop the heat to the lowest setting, keeping the rice covered
  9. After another 10 minutes, the rice should be soft to the tooth and easily fluffed.

Notes

I have run numerous experiments and am getting close to nailing down the perfect pot of rice. This still needs a little work.

Too many recipes online are vague and leave out critical information.

This aims to be an exact account of how to create the perfect fluffy rice.

It was written with the intent to serve with Asian dishes, but it works great for Mexican as well.

Steps

Tim's Cookie Dough Cookies

Ingredients

  • 113 grams (1 stick) butter
  • 30 grams (2 tablespoon) sugar
  • 90 grams (1/4 cup) light brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 3 grams (1 teaspoon) vanilla
  • 160 grams (1 1/8 cup) ap flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 grams (1/2 teaspoon) soda of baking
  • 40 grams (1/4 cup) vanilla instant pudding mix
  • 130 grams (1/2 cup) chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix flour, salt, pudding mix, and soda of baking in a small bowl
  3. Cream the light brown sugar and white sugar into the butter in a second, larger bowl with a silicone spatula
  4. Mix in egg and vanilla until air incorporates into the dough, making it lighter
  5. Barely mix the dry into the wet
  6. Gently fold in chocolate chips
  7. (Optional) Refrigerate the dough for at least 4 hours to improve flavor and texture
  8. Measure the dough and divide into 12-14 cookies (40-50g per cookie recommended)
  9. Evenly space the balls on the cookie sheet
  10. Bake the cookies for 12 minutes, or until the bottoms are barely golden brown
  11. Quickly move the cookies from the sheet pan onto a cooling rack lined with parchment

Notes

The cookies should appear slightly undercooked, do not put them back in the oven

This is an original recipe. I wanted a cookie that stayed soft for days in a row and felt like eating barely set cookie dough.

Vegetarian Chili

Ingredients

  • 36 ounces soy chorizo
  • 24 ounces diced tomatoes
  • 24 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce (unseasoned)
  • 15 ounces pinto beans
  • 15 ounces black beans
  • 16 ounces kidney beans
  • 9 ounces white onion
  • 16 ounces red bell pepper
  • 16 ounces green bell pepper
  • 8 ounces celery
  • 10 ounces carrot
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon msg (accent)
  • 1 ounce william's original chili seasoning
  • 1 splash cooking oil (peanut)
  • 1 bag corn chip scoops (optional)
  • 1 container plain greek yogurt (optional)

Steps

  1. Dice the carrots, sweet peppers, celery, and white onion. I dice each chunk of carrot and pepper to twice the volume of the celery and onion
  2. Add the diced aromatics to a large cold pot
  3. Splash the veggies with the cooking oil
  4. Sprinkle the salt, ground black pepper, garlic powder, and MSG into the pot
  5. Give everything a stir and bring the heat up to sweat the vegetables
  6. While sweating, remove the soy chorizo from its packaging and have at the ready
  7. Once a carrot is about half way to the doneness you prefer, stir in the soy chorizo. I look for the carrot to be just barely starting to soften to a fork
  8. Open and rinse the pinto, black, and kidney beans. Skipping the bean rinse will result in an unpleasant earthy flavor in the final prepped chili
  9. After the soy chorizo is brought to temperature and the pot begins sizzling again, stir in the rinsed beans
  10. Stir in the tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, and diced tomatoes
  11. Stir in the chili seasoning
  12. Simmer for at least ten minutes before serving
  13. Best results will be obtained by allowing the chili to cool and then storing in the fridge at least overnight
  14. This chili goes great with a side of corn chips and a dollop of plain greek yogurt
